Tic-Tac-Toe!

Play the Classic!

Rules of the Game:

  • Objective:

    Be the first player to get three of your symbols (X or O) in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

  • Setup:

    The game is played on a 3x3 grid.

  • Turns:

    Players take turns placing their symbol in an empty square.

  • Winning:

    The first player to align three of their symbols in a row, column, or diagonal wins.

  • Draw:

    If all squares are filled and no one has three in a row, the game ends in a draw.

That's it. Simple, fun, and strategic!
